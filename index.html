San Diego's Python Community

Discussing common Python-related interests to encourage, inspire, and share knowledge

Upcoming events

There are a number of events happening almost every week. Our general meetup is usually the last Thursday of the month. For up-to-date information, please check our meetup page.

Hire a Pythonista

Are you looking to hire a Python developer in San Diego? You can get your posting in front of over 400 members of the San Diego Python community for free. Join the Meetup group and send a description of your opening to our mailing list. You must send the email from the same address used to register with Meetup. Please specify whether the job is freelance or full-time and try to give as much detail as possible. Be concise and respectful.

Your Python project

In addition to hosting periodic project days, we have a Google group setup for getting local help on your problem. You can also bring a tough problem to the monthly general meeting as well.

Sponsors

We would like to thank the companies and organizations who have hosted or sponsored past events. Let us know if you would like to become a sponsor or host by emailing sandiegopython@gmail.com. We have worked with the Python Software Foundation to accept contributions on our behalf. Donations entirely go to hosting meetups and workshops and paying for a venue.
